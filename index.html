Summary Report

The budget impact analysis evaluates the financial implications of adopting Novel Biologic Therapy for a target population of 2,500 patients with the condition.

Key Findings:

  • The total budget impact over 5 years is estimated at $4.2 million.
  • This represents an incremental cost of $1,680 per patient.
  • The new intervention is projected to reduce hospitalizations by 720 and outpatient visits by 2,700 over the time horizon.
  • Sensitivity analysis shows the results are most sensitive to variations in the intervention cost and adoption rates.

Recommendations:

  • Consider implementing the new therapy in phases to manage budget impact.
  • Negotiate pricing agreements to reduce the intervention cost.
  • Monitor real-world adoption rates and adjust projections accordingly.
  • Evaluate clinical benefits alongside financial impacts for a complete assessment.
